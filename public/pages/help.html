Help & Tips

Country selection

The country you select does not need to be included in your keywords. It is automatically handled by the system.

Advanced filtering

Social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc.), as well as `.org`, `.int`, and other non-commercial domains, are automatically excluded to keep results relevant.

Scrape button

Once you press the Scrape button, the process starts, but there is no visual indicator unless you check the browser console. Please be patient while the scraper runs.


Scraping process

The scraper automatically navigates websites, looking for contact pages, links, shopping carts, and spans where phone numbers might be hidden.


Downloading Results

The Download CSV button should only be clicked once the results are displayed. If nothing is shown, wait for the scraping to finish.

Making sure it works

Ensure pop-ups and downloads are enabled in your browser, as the CSV file will be downloaded automatically.
